Play Snakes & Ladders

(Maximum of 4 Players)

Instructions & Rules

Add a player, then roll the dice and make the first move. Once everyone has made a character AND made their first move, you can continue to play Snakes & Ladders normally. Good Luck!

  • All players should roll once and add their roll correctly
  • Press submit to see if any snakes or ladders have activated; make the following adjustments to players' tiles.
  • Press the submit button and make all changes BEFORE ending the turn
  • To win the game, you must reach EXACTLY 100
  • Going over 100 results in going backwards the same amount.
  • Ex. Rolling 6 when you are at Tile 95 would bring you to 99. Rolling 10 at Tile 99 would bring you to 91.
  • Upon hitting 100, please refrain from changing your score. Play until three players hit Tile 100.
